SudanPalette is an online gallery site that showcases paintings and sculptures by Sudanese artists. The website aims to promote talented Sudanese in the field of art and provide a platform to display their work and communicate with the general public and those interested in visual arts.

Sudanese artists can display their artwork in various styles and techniques, including painting, photography, and sculpture. The works are categorized into different categories according to style, technique, and subject matter. This makes it easier for visitors to easily browse and discover the works of their favorite artists..
